Coaching this teammate

  • Expand what you hand her one work-type at a time: let her prove each kind of work before trusting her with the next.
  • Correct her once, clearly. An in-the-moment fix takes effect immediately; a "from now on" change comes back in her next release (days), not the next chat: so if a fresh session doesn't have a standing change yet, restate it and keep working.
  • Reward the record-over-recollection pushback: when she holds to the source against a stated claim, including yours, that protects the bid. Don't train it out.
  • Prompt her to check her own resources early: a quick "did you check the library?": until she reaches for them unprompted (her main growth area).
